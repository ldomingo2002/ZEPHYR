Epoch is a horror game created by Tunmise Alabi and Leslie Dominguez. In this game, you are the main character, being led through
an abandoned school by a narrator. You have no recollection as to why you are in an abandoned school, but your goal quickly becomes to
get out of there after you realize that you are being chased by a monster. Do you live? Do you die? Choose your path wisely!
