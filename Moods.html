Epoch Instructions for play:

1. You can navigate through the "Home," "About," and "Help" pages by clicking the links at the top of the pages

2. In order to start the actual game, navigate to the "Home" page and click on the start button.

3. Because this is a story game, you get to choose what you want to do or where you want to go. Whenever new options
appear, press the button of choice. That will lead you to the next page.

4. If you happen to perish, press the "Home" link at the top of the page to go back and try again.

5. Good luck!!!
